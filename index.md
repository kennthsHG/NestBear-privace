# NestBear Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 23, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **NestBear** (package name: KeepNest; hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

**Core Value**: A local-first home inventory app that helps you track what you own, where items are stored, quantities, prices, and expiration dates — with browsing, statistics, checklists, and reminders. No account registration is required.

---

## 2. Information Collection

### 2.1 Information We Collect

The App may store the following types of information on your device:

| Information Type | Purpose | Required |
|-----------------|---------|----------|
| Inventory Items | Name, quantity, unit, price, location, category, status, tags, notes, expiration date | Yes (Core Feature) |
| Item Photos | Images attached to inventory items (manual entry, photo entry, batch entry) | No |
| Item Change Log | History of edits to inventory records | Yes (Core Feature) |
| Storage Metadata | Locations, categories, statuses, tags, and units you define | Yes (Core Feature) |
| Checklists | Checklist titles, items, and completion state | Yes (Core Feature) |
| App Preferences | Theme, language, home layout, expiry alert lead time, search bar visibility | No |
| Subscription Status | Pro subscription type and expiry (managed by the platform store via RevenueCat) | No |
| Export Files | CSV exports you generate and share from the device | No |

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identification information
- Your precise location or GPS coordinates
- Your contacts or social relationship data
- Your financial or payment card information
- Device advertising identifiers for ad targeting
- Analytics or crash-reporting profiles tied to your identity

---

## 3. Permissions

### 3.1 Camera Permission

**Purpose**: Allows you to take photos of inventory items when adding or editing items.

**Required**: No. Denying this permission does not affect other features; you can still add items without photos or choose images from your library.

### 3.2 Photo Library Permission

**Purpose**: Allows you to select existing photos from your album for inventory items.

**Required**: No. Denying this permission allows normal use of other features.

### 3.3 In-App Purchases / Billing

**Purpose**: Allows subscription purchase and restoration through Apple App Store or Google Play.

**Required**: No. The free tier remains usable without a subscription.

### 3.4 Notifications (Future)

Push notification settings are planned for a future update. The current version does not request notification permission and does not deliver push notifications.

---

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Local Storage

- All inventory data (items, photos metadata, locations, categories, checklists, settings) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
- Item photos are stored in the app's local file storage on your device
- We do not upload your inventory content to our own servers
- Uninstalling the App will delete local data; recovery depends on your system backup (iCloud / Google Backup)

### 4.2 Offline-First Design

The App is designed to work offline:

- Item entry and editing: Works completely offline
- Browse, search, and filters: Works completely offline
- Statistics and reports: Works completely offline
- Checklists and calendar views: Works completely offline
- CSV export: Works offline; sharing uses your device's share sheet

### 4.3 Cloud Sync

The App is local-first. Multi-user or real-time cloud sync is not enabled in the current version. If cloud sync is introduced in the future, we will update this policy and seek your consent before enabling it.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

- Data is stored within the app sandbox, protected by the operating system's security mechanisms
- Inventory content is not transmitted to our servers by default
- We recommend using system backup features (iCloud Backup / Google Backup) to protect your data

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this App without authorization, please contact us.

## 8. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you actively delete it, move it to Trash, or uninstall the App
- **Subscription Data**: Managed by the platform store and RevenueCat, subject to their respective retention policies

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all inventory records, checklists, and settings within the App at any time
- **Delete**: Delete individual items, empty Trash, reset data in settings, or uninstall the App to remove local data
- **Export**: Export inventory data to CSV and share or save it through your device
- **Manage Subscriptions**: Manage or cancel your Pro subscription in the Apple App Store or Google Play account settings
